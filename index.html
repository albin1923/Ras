§ 01 — WELCOME

Welcome!

CHAPTER VISION

The IEEE Robotics & Automation Society Student Branch Chapter at Saintgits is a home for students who want to build, research and ship real robotics — from small autonomous rovers to industrial automation and applied AI.

We host technical talks, workshops, hackathons and hands-on project sprints.
Members work across hardware, firmware, perception, control and machine learning — with the shared goal of doing engineering that actually runs.
